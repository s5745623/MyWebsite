The code and the dataset: Click Here

The visualization is created in ggplot2/R, The first graph is comparing the different continent and year with the death count showing as size and color. There are three peaks of the highest death count, which are Sub Saharan Africa, North America and Middle East & North Africa.

The second graph is comparing the attack type and target type with the same legend. The top 3 are Hostage Taking of Military, Hijacking of Private citizens, and armed assault to private citizens.

The last graph is an easier way to show the death count by year. In the year of 2014 clearly shows that terrorism was very active.
