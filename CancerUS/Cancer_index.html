    Leafelt/R

Leaflet

The visualization is generated using Leaflet/R.

As the basis of this interactive map, US cancer and breast cancer rate has been added (shown as number out of 100,000) which can toggle on the top right.

The heat map like map show higher rate of the breast cancer and cancer, the gray represents no data.

The marker can also be toggled, with the option of `Land Use Sites, Hospitals, and Nuclear Power Plants`.

The nuclear Power plant tend the be on the east and west coast, and is highly correlated to the cancer rated. Interestingly the cancer rate is higher on the east coast while breast cancer rate is high on the west coast.
