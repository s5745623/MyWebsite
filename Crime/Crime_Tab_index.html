Tableau

This visualization is produced using Tableau with the data of crime in Montgomery, MD.

The compound visualization owns a map separated by day of weeks, follow by the graph showing the top crime occurred by day of week which is DUI happened the most(especially weekends).

The bottom left bar graph is the overall TOP10 crime count with the most frequent DUI follow by CDS-POSS Marijuana.

The bottom right graph is done by taking the top three crime and separated by day of week and hours. Where DUI mostly happened around weekend nights; POL information are in the weekdays
