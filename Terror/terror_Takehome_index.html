US Terrorism Analysis

When and where the terrorism happened the most in the past 46 years in US?

In the modern world, Terrorism is a problem of the society of this era. This analysis will show the distrbution of terrorism activity of the past 46 years.

Also the visualizations will show the trend of the terrorism growth or decrease. The story here will tell how terror attacks varies from 1970 to 2016 and among 50 states in the US when and where the tragedies took place.

World Terrorism HeatMap - click

The visualization is created using Plotly to show the map of terrorism attacks took place from 1970 to 2016, which is 46 years.

The timeline can be drag by each year which is also done by Plotly function slider. The circle represents the number of death. Also the when hover the point, will show the country, attack type, target type, and the death count.

Terror attack start become active around the mid 70s, which took place all around the world and unpredictable. Interestingly, Australia is the continent which had the least terror attack.

The size of the bubble stands for the number of death of the terrorism attack with the death number larger and equals to 1.
