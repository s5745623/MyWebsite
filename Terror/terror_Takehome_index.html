US Terrorism Analysis

When and where the terrorism happened the most in the past 46 years in US?

In the modern world, Terrorism is a problem of the society of this era. This analysis will show the distrbution of terrorism activity of the past 46 years.

Also the visualizations will show the trend of the terrorism growth or decrease. The story here will tell how terror attacks varies from 1970 to 2016.

The DataSet: Click Here

World Terrorism HeatMap - click

The visualization is created using Plotly to show the map of terrorism attacks took place from 1970 to 2016, which is 46 years.

The timeline can be drag by each year which is also done by Plotly function slider. The circle represents the number of death. Also the when hover the point, will show the country, attack type, target type, and the death count.

Terror attack start become active around the mid 70s, which took place all around the world and unpredictable. Interestingly, Australia is the continent which had the least terror attack.

The size of the bubble stands for the number of death of the terrorism attack with the death number larger and equals to 1.

US Terrorism Map

Let's take a further look at the US Terrorism activities. The bubble represents the death count by size and also represents as the number of events which included death count equals to 0.

The visualization is created using Plotly to show the map of terrorism attacks took place in the US from 1970 to 2016, which is 46 years.

The timeline can be drag by each year which is also done by Plotly function slider. The circle represents the number of death. Also the when hover the point, will show the country, states, attack type, target type, group name, and the death count.

In the year of 1996 and 2001 there are several events with large bubble.

US Terrorism Map with hospital and population density - click

The visualization is generated using Leaflet/R.

After look through the temporal change in the terror events, further analysis has been done by mapping the entrie US terrorism activities for 46 years. As for the toggle of this interactive map, US population density(people / mi2) and hopitals location has been added which can toggle on the top right.

The heat map like map show higher population density. With most of the terrorism activities mostly occured in high population density area. Except Hawaii only occured once while having a high population density.

The visualization is created in superheat/R, heat map graph is showing the network of the Marvel Universe Hero.

Original dataset contains 547768 edges and 19091 nodes. In the connection of edges it include the interaction of characters within the comic books and other medias, so I chose the edges that only appear in the other media; and use the network`s `from and to` to build an unique dataset of nodes. After cleaned the dataset, it still contained 224182 of edges, so I narrow down the edges to 50 edges, also took an unique list for the nodes.

The heatmap visualize the connections between characters(the data has been sorted) while it appears that the top and strongest connection is a character name the `PATRIOT`, and he seems like have an strong relationship of himself.

The heatmap includes a scale legend and a dendrogram of rows and columns.
