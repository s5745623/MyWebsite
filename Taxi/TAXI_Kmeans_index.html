Bokeh

The graph is using bokeh the show the popular TAXI pick ups in NYC.

The kmean clustering by using k starting from 1 to 20 , and drew the centroids on the Google Map to have a better visualization.

For the maps below, 1, 5 , 10 , 15 and 20 are selected to be the number of centroids k. Nearly, all the centroids were accumulated in Manhattan, JFK and LGA; as for Newark airport, the centroid appeared around Newark airport when k was equal to 15.
